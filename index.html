About Me


Hi, my name is Ryan Burke. I currently work as a freelance data scientist, but I think of myself as an explorer and story-teller. I fell in love with data more than a decade ago. It's an amazing feeling to sail through an ocean of data! Sometimes it's messy and choppy. Other times it's smooth sailing. Yet, with focus and a child-like curiosity, I can always find a treasure!

During my free time, I really enjoy learning. I'm constantly working on aquiring and refining new skills. I will showcase some of my projects here, and link them to their GitHub repositories.
